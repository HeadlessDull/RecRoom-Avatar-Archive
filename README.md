# Current Blender Tools

We've made an addon to easily recreate your Rec Room Avatar with just a few clicks, now with prop support!

> 📦 Find it in [Releases](https://github.com/HeadlessDull/RecRoom-Avatar-Archive/releases)

## The Rec Room Rig

When opening the addon you will get a button to setup your scene for the rig:

<div align="center">
  <img width="242" height="93" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/15710c7a-22f7-4e61-aa94-1d35203b9a2c" />
</div>

With this rig you get support for both Full Body and Modern Bean Avatars, fully rigged and ready to animate, even with a Rig UI:
> edited from the offical [Rec Room Avatar Tools](https://github.com/RecRoomGame/rr_avatar_tools).

<div align="center">
  <img width="800" alt="rigui" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dd70ae2a-a962-46a9-805b-e549c49c96ec" />
  <p><em>Full Body and Bean Body avatar support with Rig UI</em></p>
</div>

## Item Search and Appending

Under the rig setup you can load in avatar items from this GitHub repo without ever opening your browser

> You get to go through specific item slots you may need or search the item you are wanting
>> ⚠️ Projects with large amount of items can cause large storage sizes

<div align="center">
  <img width="229" height="530" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/473e68f5-9695-44a1-a50c-250d13b98305" />
</div>

These items come pre-rigged for your ease of use. Pose, animate, and render with them however you want. Once appended they are saved locally on your computer.

<div align="center">
  <img width="800" alt="items" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/95d97444-c312-460b-a9df-dde2f0b87bcc" />
  <p><em>Appended items ready to pose and animate</em></p>
</div>

## Unity Export (BETA)

With this feature you can export your avatar from Blender into an FBX ready for Unity and VRChat.

<div align="center">
  <img width="335" height="145" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f4bd5b7c-1c38-428a-aca0-995473e69fb8" />
</div>

This will also convert textures to be Unity-compatible without special shaders (includes Albedo and Normal Map):

<div align="center">
  <img width="800" alt="textures" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0a78f754-984f-4c1b-a67f-de34ea9a8d88" />
  <p><em>Blender addon texture (left) vs exported texture (right)</em></p>
</div>

<div align="center">
  <img width="800" alt="mesh comparison" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e9fafeee-3c18-4631-b76c-a9ad67766a09" />
  <p><em>Original mesh (left) vs exported mesh (right) in Blender</em></p>
</div>

**Known Limitations:**
> ⚠️ The face will not be included in the export
>
> ⚠️ Both body types must be enabled at the same time during export
>
> ⚠️ Props get exported without textures
>
> ℹ️ Bean Body exports with the Full Body mesh to meet Unity's rig requirements, you can delete it afterward



# The Archive itself

A large collection of images, textures, and FBX files covering most avatar items and Props used in-game.

> ⚠️ Does not include UGC community items or known collab items
>
> ℹ️ Community-submitted UGC assets may be accepted in the future
>
> ⚠️ Not all items are Blender-ready yet, we are actively working on expanding compatibility

Can't find the item you're looking for in the addon? Browse the full raw file list here:

📄 [RRO Item List (Google Sheets)](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1QyqQu747pG7JVD1dUIFo_XpPXL-EfzXkJfWW1JXGw5w/edit?usp=sharing)
